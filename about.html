About

We bring an innovative and refreshing approach to ERP initiatives.

ERP projects have always been difficult, and they still are. At ERP Technology Partners, we strive to help our clients find the light at the end of the tunnel. Through a successful ERP transformation, we guide our clients to become more profitable, experience tremendous growth, and gain a competitive advantage in their respective industries.

Backed by decades of business and technology leadership, ERP Technology Partners helps our clients actualize the benefits available through an ERP transformation. Our approach to implementation begins with understanding the purpose of a technology transformation and then mapping a plan to realize the needed benefits. We help our clients to design an implementation plan that is simple, effective, and affordable.

Specializing in oil & gas, utilities, aerospace & defense, manufacturing & distribution, engineering & construction,  management, telecommunications, and services industries, our combination of technical and functional expertise brings a very direct and transparent alternative to the “old-school” consulting programs.

Our leadership each brings 20+ years in the technology and advisory space, having experience ranging from the “Big5,” to boutique consulting firms, to direct industry background. Our average consulting tenure exceeds 10 years, leveraged between consulting background and direct industry experience. We all love what we do, and would welcome a conversation with anyone facing an upcoming ERP initiative.
